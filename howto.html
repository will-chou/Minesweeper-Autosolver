HOW TO PLAY

Objective

The objective of minesweeper is to avoid all the mines in the board, and to reveal all the safe squares.  The game starts with a grid of cells that either have a hidden mine or is a safe cell.  By using logic and probability, the user moves through the grid, revealing free cells and avoiding mines.  Once all the safe cells are revealed, the game is won.

Types of Cells
